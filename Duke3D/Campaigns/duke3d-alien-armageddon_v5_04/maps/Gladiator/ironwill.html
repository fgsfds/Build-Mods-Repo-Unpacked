Discription : During the construction of Sewerside (Singleplayer map) I builded a symmetric room which was actually better to use for a deathmatchmap so I took it out and developed it further as a multiplayer level. It features an Unreal Tournament-like construction and, like stated earlier, is symmetric just like many of my deathmatchlevels.

Suitable for up to 4 players.
