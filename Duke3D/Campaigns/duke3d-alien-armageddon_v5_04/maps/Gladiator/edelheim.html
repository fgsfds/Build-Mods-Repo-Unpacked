Description : "Castle Edelheim" is a deathmatchmap taking place in a medival castle. An extra texture-file is enclosed with the map which is required ofcourse. It can be played without a port of Duke3D and is suitable for 2 to 4 players. Eight players is also possible although I wouldn't recommend it. The castle's walls are accessible as well as the six guardtowers.

Next to this, it's exactly ten years ago that the demo of Duke Nukem 3D was released. Happy birthday Duke!
