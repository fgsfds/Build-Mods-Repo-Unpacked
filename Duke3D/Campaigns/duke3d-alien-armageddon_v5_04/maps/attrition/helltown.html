Discription : Helltown is a singleplayermap in a city environment, as the name already gives away. This map took a very long time to complete but the result is much worth it. A hard but fun gameplay awaits the player when he fights his way throughout the wrecked streets of Helltown, looking for all the aliens who invaded the town. This map comes with an .art-file containing an extra texture which is needed to play Helltown.

Warning !
You can not play this map with the original Duke Nukem 3D Atomic Edition anymore. This because I have used more sectors then the original can handle. You must use the JonoFport of Duke Nukem 3D Atomic Edition in order to play Helltown.
