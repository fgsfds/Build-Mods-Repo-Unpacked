Penthouse
Created By: Michael_Hunt

Premise:
Follow-up to  Penthouse. After leaving his penthouse, Dukes chopper is shot down near the entrance to the offshore facility he was heading for and it becomes apparent that someone else got there first...

Objective:
Access the facility and search for survivors.

Planned to be level 3 of 5; other levels in various stages of development, currently no planned completion date.
