Penthouse
Created By: Michael_Hunt

Premise:
Follow-up to BankJob. After escaping into the subway, Duke needs to make his way to his penthouse and make contact with the EDF. However, the station is locked down and he needs to find another way out...

Objective:
Reach Dukes rooftop penthouse and board an EDF helicopter.

Known Glitches:
Mirror in hotel room can show sprties on other side of the wall when viewed from the right angle.
Textures in penthouse lift glitch slightly when elevator is moving.
Neither effect gameplay.

Planned to be level 2 of 5; other levels in various stages of development, currently no planned completion date.
